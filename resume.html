How It Works

Submit your résumé, your Tufts email, and any additional notes you may have in the form at the right. One of the Halligan Careers staff will try to reach out to you promptly with feedback.

If you have any particular companies you are striving for, feel free to include this in the notes section. We'll try to connect your résumé with a Tufts student at the company so that they can give you targeted feedback.
